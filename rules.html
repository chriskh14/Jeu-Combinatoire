Duolitaire Game

The Rules

You have a grid with cell.Each cell has a definite number called " weight" and a "value". Weights are static while numbers can be moved and switched with these intructions:

- One can only move to a blank cell next to a full cell and must not move over an adjacent cell wether it's empty or not

- One must move until the end of his/her turn

- Each player gets his/her own area

- The score is calculcated by the number of the cell times its' own weight per area

- The area with the lowest score wins the game
